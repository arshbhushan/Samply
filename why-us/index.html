Samply: Specialists in Product Sampling, Customer Engagement, and Strategic Alliances

Contact Us

We design impactful sampling campaigns that are targeted, engaging, and cost-effective. Our innovative approach combines alternative media, activations, and experiential marketing to ensure measurable results and meaningful customer interactions.

Requirement Briefing

We conceptualize ideas and prepare campaign briefs based on the demographics such as gender and age, psychographics such as attitude and perception, and geography, in alignment with the requirements of your brand.

Tailored Strategies

We develop personalized plans to meet your specific needs, outlining timelines and budgets. Our approach helps identify ideal partners for strategic alliances and ensures your campaigns reach the right audience.

Logistics Management

We handle your stock, overseeing the entire logistics of sample distribution. From secure packaging and safe handling to efficient storage, transportation, and final distribution, we ensure every step is managed effectively.

Campaign Management

We excel in understanding brands and know how to successfully manage and deliver campaigns to boost sales. Our integrated dashboards provide timely updates, ensuring you stay informed every step of the way.

Audit Services

Thorough audits are essential for detecting and preventing theft. Our platform ensures zero pilferage by tracking each sample to the consumer, providing comprehensive final reports that enhance campaign effectiveness.

Feedback

We provide customized activity reports for all campaigns, featuring verifiable feedback from consumers and our team. Additionally, we utilize QR codes on samples for extra feedback insights.
